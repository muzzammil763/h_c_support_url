Welcome to the HandyCurls support page! HandyCurls is a marketplace app connecting stylists and clients for easy hair service booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HandyCurls?
HandyCurls is a mobile app that connects clients with hair stylists in their area. It allows clients to easily find, book, and pay for hair services. Stylists can use the app to manage their appointments, showcase their work, and grow their client base.
How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, simply browse stylists in your area, view their profiles, and select a service and time that works for you. You can then securely book and pay through the app.

How do I contact a stylist?
You can contact a stylist directly through the app's messaging feature. You can discuss your specific needs and preferences before booking an appointment.
I'm a stylist. How do I sign up?
Stylists can create a profile directly within the HandyCurls app. Provide your information, showcase your skills, and set your availability to start accepting bookings.
What if I have a problem with my appointment?
Please contact our support team using the email address below. We will work to resolve any issues you may encounter. We also encourage you to contact the stylist directly to discuss your concerns.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing.
First, try closing and restarting the app. If that doesn't work, try restarting your device. If the problem persists, please contact support with details about your device and what you were doing when the app crashed.
I can't log in.
Make sure you're using the correct email address and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. If you're still having trouble, contact support.
